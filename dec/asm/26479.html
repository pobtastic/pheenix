26479: Print String
Used by the routines at PrintScore, PrintHeader, Print_ControlMenu, Print_InstructionsMenu, Handler_PlayerDeathGameOver, Handler_DisplayMothershipBonus, Handler_AlienExplosions and TitleScreen.

This routine differs from SpectrumFont_PrintString and CustomFont_PrintString as the code self-modifies the address pointed to by IX, whereas the other two routines are always pointed to 24915 and 15360.
